why pos_connect ?

pos_connect is an anonymous social networking point of sale system. It silently connects you with other businesses to share non-sensitive product data, which give you knowledge about new product and help you import them in a fast and reliable manner. For sensitive data, which is profit information exchange that help you maximize profit, you will have an option to participate in the data sharing features. It is simple and easy to use. You have the fexibility to manage your business online. pos_connect works both online and offline so that you don't need a reliable and fast internet to use. Your database is power by IBM and Amazon and we can trust them to keep our database secure

Product information exchange



When you carry a new product, you have to define its information for the first time. With pos_connect, there is a chance that other members have already defined this information. pos_connect combines all members data and filter out the most popular information to help you import new product. In return, your product information will help other members in the same way. (3:25)


There is a less common case where product information exchange help you to add new sku to your existing product (3:04)

Profit Information Exchange (PIE) (3:01)



Members who participate in PIE can anonymously help each other to maximize profit. If you don't like to paticipate in PIE, your data will not be exposed to others to help them maximize their profit, and in return, you will not recieve other PIE members' data to maximize yours

For a product, pos_connect can show the relationship of cost, price, volumn, and profit from PIE members. PIE members who have a similar product cost as you and make more profit than you can suggest you to lower or raise your price to be more profitable like them.

pos_connect can sort out a list of top profitable products that many PIE members are carrying to suggest you.

Online business management



pos_connect give you a flexibility to control your business anywhere with online access. You can remotely view sale data, change tax rate, product price, or promotional deal ... (2:11)

Internet connection reliability



To make pos_connect more reliable with your day-to-day sale operations, the sale application operates offline by donwloading your product database to your local browser. Sale data is saved offline first and periodically reminded to be upload online later when the internet is available. This make pos_connect fast and reliable like other non-web software. (4:52)

Data security



Most pos store its database on your local computer and you are responsible to create back up, keep them up_to_date, and restore it when something go wrong.

pos_connect is power by IBM and Amazon servers. These cooperation have the resource to take care all the boring backup task for us. If your computer crash, simply replace it with a new one, connect it to the internet and you are done because your data is online.
